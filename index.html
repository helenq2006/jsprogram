Hot and Cold

Objective: You have limited turns to guess a secret number, starting at 50.
You will be informed how close you are based on Hot and Cold.
If you get the answer within five guesses, you win. Otherwise, you lose.
You have the option of choosing to add or subtract by 1s, 5s, 10s, and 25s.
If you are "Very Hot" and guess incorrectly, the guesses remaining will not be subtracted.
