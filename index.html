Hot & Cold

Objective: You have 5 turns to guess a secret number, starting at 50.
The computer will tell you how close you are to the number,
with responses ranging from Very Cold.... to Very Hot!!!!
You have the option of adding or subtracting by 1s, 5s, 10s, and 25s.
Guesses remaining will not be subtracted if you are Very Hot.
